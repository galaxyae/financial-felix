Our Products

Short and Medium Term depository Notes​​

We have developed an array of depository notes in both Nigerian Naira and US Dollars. These notes have tenures of either 182 days, 364 days or 728 days, offering periodic payments of returns while ensuring your principal is returned to the account holder at maturity

Purchase of Government Securities

We process the purchase of select government securities ranging from federal and state bonds, treasury bills and select debenture notes that are issued by the government
