Description

We’ve made investing in Nigerian stocks and fixed assets very easy to help you reach your financial goals from the comfort of your phone.



The Blue Source Investment app is designed to give you control of your finances with innovative real time information that you can use to make sound financial decisions. You can easily view your portfolio by asset class or see the performance of your entire portfolio or particular asset over time.



Invest in quality stocks in the Nigerian Stock Exchange, whether you have a hold strategy to profit in the long run or wish to take advantage of current opportunities, you are well equipped to earn good returns from local investments.



Protect your money by investing in low-risk assets like Fixed Income where returns are historically guaranteed.



You can create goals, set personalized financial targets, and achieve them through automatic deposits while enjoying competitive interest rates.



You also have access to easy payments and withdrawals from and to Nigerian bank accounts and seamless bills and utilities payments and airtime purchase



*Important Note: This app is for use by those who have a Nigerian issued Bank Verification Number. It can only be used with a Nigerian bank account.
